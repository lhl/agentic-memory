# Punchlist — Academic / Industry Memory Papers

*Created: 2026-02-22*

This punchlist tracks our work to produce:

1. **Archived paper artifacts** (PDF + optional text snapshot) under `references/papers/`
2. A **reference summary** per paper under `references/*.md` (YAML frontmatter; mechanism-first)
3. A **root-level deep dive** per paper under `ANALYSIS-*.md` (synthesis-ready)
4. A final synthesis doc: `ANALYSIS-academic-industry.md`

Conventions (see `templates/README.md`):
- PDFs: `references/papers/arxiv-<id>.pdf` (git-lfs tracked via `*.pdf`)
- Optional text snapshot: `references/papers/arxiv-<id>.md` (usually `pdftotext` output)
- Reference summary: `references/<firstauthor>-<shortslug>.md`
- Analysis: `ANALYSIS-arxiv-<id>-<shortslug>.md`
